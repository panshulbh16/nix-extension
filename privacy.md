Overview
Nix ("the extension") is a browser cache and data cleaner for Google Chrome. This privacy policy describes how the extension handles user data.
Data Collection
Nix does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or non-personal data. No information of any kind leaves your device.
Permissions
Nix requests one permission: browsingData. This permission is used solely to delete browser data (cache, cookies, history, etc.) when you explicitly click "Clear Now." The extension cannot and does not read your browsing data — it only removes it.
Third-Party Services
Nix does not use analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or any third-party services. There is no backend server.
